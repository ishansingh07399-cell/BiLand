Cultural Highlights

Classical Dance

Vibrant forms like Jat-Jatin and Bidesia bring Bihar’s folk energy to life.

Traditional Attire

Women wear Madhubani-printed sarees, while men don Dhoti-Kurta during festivals.

Cultural Fairs

Chhath Puja and Sonepur Mela are major events showcasing devotion and tradition.
